More Than Just Soup

Hulatang is a traditional Chinese spicy soup that has been the heart of Central China's breakfast culture for centuries.

  • Origin: Henan Province, Central China
  • History: Over 800 years of tradition
  • Cultural Significance: Daily morning ritual
  • Character: Spicy, savory, complex flavors
