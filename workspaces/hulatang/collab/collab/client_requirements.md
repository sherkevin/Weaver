# Hulatang Promotional PPT Requirements

## Project Overview
Create a promotional PowerPoint presentation for Hulatang (胡辣汤), a traditional Chinese spicy soup, to increase awareness and drive customer interest.

## PPT Theme
- **Main Theme**: "The Soul of Central China's Breakfast"
- **Focus**: Authenticity, tradition, and modern appeal
- **Target Audience**: Young adults (20-40), food enthusiasts, cultural explorers

## Page Requirements
- **Total Pages**: 12-15 slides
- **Duration**: 10-12 minute presentation

## Style Requirements
- **Color Scheme**: Warm reds, earthy browns, and vibrant oranges (reflecting the soup's spicy nature)
- **Typography**: Modern sans-serif for headings, traditional Chinese font elements for cultural touch
- **Visual Style**: High-quality food photography with clean, minimalist layout
- **Mood**: Appetizing, energetic, culturally rich

## Content Structure

## Special Requirements
- **Language**: Bilingual (Chinese and English) where appropriate
- **Interactive Elements**: QR codes for social media and online ordering
- **Animation**: Subtle transitions between slides
- **Audio**: Optional background music suggestion (traditional Chinese instrumental)
- **Mobile Optimization**: Ensure readability on tablets and phones
